These animals need your help!

HELP US MAKE A BETTER WORLD FOR THE ANIMALS

Every living thing in this world needed each other to survive. Humans need animals, animals need humans, plants need animals. Basically all living things depend on each other. The Malaysian Wildlife are trying to undo any damaged done by human throughout the Malaysia as well as internationally. Our future generation would be able to see and learn all about the various wildlife and species throughout the world.

People who care about wildlife are very welcomed to donate through our website by bank transfer. All money collected will contribute greatly to the animal ecosystem. Your donations will be a big step forward in maintaining and improving the wildlife in Malaysia.

The Malaysian Wildlife would like to express our gratitude to the volunteers, donors and adopters for contributing in making the world a better place for the animals.
