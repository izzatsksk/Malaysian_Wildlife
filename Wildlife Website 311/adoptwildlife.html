ADOPT WILDLIFE

Help us in feeding these animals!


ADOPT AN ORANGUTAN

The orangutan is the most human-like primate and its DNA is 97% the same to ours. An orangutan's diet consists of 90% fruit and their favourite fruit is durians. Although they mostly eat fruits, they still require other nutrients by eating leaves and nuts. The costs to rehabilitate an orangutan is RM8,000 per year. Lets help our "cousins" to live a better and happy life!


ADOPT AN TIGER

A Malayan tiger eats around 6kg of meat everyday. They need to eat sufficiently in order to be strong and healthy. It costs RM100 per day to feed a tiger. RM40,000 is needed to feed a tiger annually as well as its medicines. Why don't you lend a helping hand in keeping our tigers healthy?
