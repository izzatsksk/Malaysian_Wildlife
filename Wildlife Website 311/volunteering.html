Come and volunteer in these exciting activities to help us make wildlife in Malaysia better!


VOLUNTEERING ACTIVITIES



ORANGUTAN

Become a volunteer at the Matang Wildlife Center. Perform various activities such as husbandry, construction, organic farming and many more. Our beloved Borneo orangutans are an endangered species due to demand in illegal wildlife trade. Let's give these orangutans the life that they deserve!


PANDA

Spend the day with pandas and learn about the animals’ way of life first hand. You will have to get down and dirty and work along other volunteers on a variety of chores like cleaning panda’s cages, preparing meals and feeding the animals, planting bamboo trees, making supplies and more. You will also get to learn various amazing facts about pandas by attending classes and watching documentaries!


TURTLE

Join our team as we go to Terengganu and engage in activities such as nighttime beach patrols, egg collection and nest monitoring to help us stop the decreasing population of turtles and collect scientific data. Other than that, you can also spend your free time swimming at the beach.


TIGER

Poachers are the main cause of the decrease in tigers' population in Malaysia. Take part in becoming an anti-poacher patrol team and record animal tracks such as footprints and scratch marks. You can also go jungle trekking, caving and meet the orang asli.
