MALAYSIAN WILDLIFE

The Malaysian Wildlife is a non profit organization based on Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Established since 2012 by the Founder / President of the organization, Zahril Ahmad Iman with the purpose of protecting and conserving the natural habitat of wildlife across Malaysia as well as internationally through voluntary programmes. As wildlife population are decreasing over the years, this organization had to step up to the game to prevent poachers doing something terrible to the wildlife habitats.

AIMS

The purpose of this endeavor is to create safe haven for wildlife to continue their existence. As wildlife does not consists of only animals, but all flora and fauna located in the wilds. Their habitats are endangered by poachers and hunters who only think of gaining profits by taking their rights to live and their habitats.

STRATEGIC PLAN

To achieve our ultimate goals, Malaysian Wildlife plans to increase volunteers across the country and held a charity events every year to get high amount of resources. Multiple framework also has been planned out to be implemented within a short period of time. This strategic plan ensures that this organization are heading into a fight where the world with wildlife can be saved
